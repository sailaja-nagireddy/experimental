Closantel

Closantel is a widely used veterinary anti helminthic drug which is active against Fasciola and Haemonchus species.

Rafoxanide

Rafoxanide is a salicylanilide used as an anthelmintic. It is most commonly used in ruminant animals to treat adult liver flukes of the species Fasciola hepatica and Fasciola gigantica.

Niclosamide

Niclosamide is a medication used to treat tapeworm infestations including diphyllobothriasis, hymenolepiasis, and taeniasis.
